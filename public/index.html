FIFA Squad Builder

The Ultimate Draft Challenge

🎯

Objective

Build the highest-rated 11-player squad before your opponents do. Score points from ratings, chemistry, captain bonus, and peer votes.

💰

Budget

Each manager starts with $1,000M. Spend wisely — leftover budget is penalised at the end of the game.

🔄

Turn Order

Turns rotate round-robin — Player 1 picks, then Player 2, then Player 3... You pick one footballer per turn then pass to the next manager.

🏟

Formations

Choose 4-3-3 Attack (CAM) or 4-3-3 Defend (DM) on your first turn. Your pitch layout adapts accordingly.

🎲

Risk Pick

Don't like your draw? Hit Risk Pick to reroll from the full pool. Limited to 2 rerolls per selection — and you won't see the same player twice.

📍

Position Penalties

Playing a player out of sub-role (e.g. CM at CAM) costs −$15M. Playing them in a different main category (e.g. defender as midfielder) costs −$30M.

⚛

Chemistry

Players sharing a club or country earn chemistry bonuses (up to +5 rating each). A cohesive squad can outscore a flashier one.

🎖

Captain Bonus

Once your squad is full, pick a captain for a permanent +2 rating boost. Choose your best player.

🖲

Trading

Sell a drawn footballer to another manager via a blind bid. The seller offers, the buyer bids, the seller accepts or rejects.

🏆

Scoring

Final score = Avg Rating + Captain Boost − Budget Penalty. Then each manager votes for the best rival squad — votes add +2 pts each.
